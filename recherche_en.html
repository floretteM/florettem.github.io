    • Research fields

    I use lattices as cryptanalysis tools, to attack existing cryptosystems. Lattice are efficient to solve linear systems, especially when the solution is small (approximation of diophantines equations, zeros of multivariate modular polynomials).
    I also try using lattices as construction tools to create new cryptographic protocols, for example identity_based encryption protocoles.

    My Ph.D

    I did a Ph.D, Mathematical study of pseudo rendom number generators under the supervision of Pr. Damien Vergnaud at Sorbonne Université, LIP6, in the ALMASTY team.
